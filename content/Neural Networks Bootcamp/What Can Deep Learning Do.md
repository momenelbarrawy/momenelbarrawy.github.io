## 1. Computer Vision
### **Object Detection**
- `Localize and classify each object in an image`.
- Example: Cars, trucks, traffic lights with bounding boxes.

### **Image Generation**
- `GANs generate realistic images (e.g., faces) from noise`.

### **CycleGANs**
- `Translate images across domains`: zebra ↔ horse, summer ↔ winter.

### **Face Aging**
- `Predict how a person will look in the future`.

### **Text-to-Image Synthesis**
- `Generate images from text descriptions`.
- Example: “A blue bird with a short beak.”

### **Sketch-to-Image**
- `Convert hand-drawn sketches into realistic images`.

### **Image Super-Resolution**
- `Enhance low-resolution images into high-resolution ones` (e.g., SRGAN, SFTGAN).

### **Image Captioning**
- `Generate natural language descriptions of images`.

### **Dense Captioning**
- `Generate detailed descriptions for individual objects in an image`.

### **Scene Graph Generation**
- `Predict relationships between objects (subject–predicate–object)`.

### **Visual Question Answering (VQA)**
- `Answer natural language questions about a given image`.

### **Segmentation**
- **Semantic Segmentation**: `Classify each pixel by category`.
- **Instance Segmentation**: `Detect and separate individual object instances (e.g., Mask R-CNN)`.

### **Image Colorization**
- `Transform grayscale images into colored images`.

### **Image Inpainting**
- `Fill missing or cropped parts of an image realistically`.

---

## 2. Natural Language Processing (NLP)
### **Machine Translation**
- `Translate text between languages` (e.g., English → German).
- `Uses sequence-to-sequence models` (encoder–decoder).

### **Chatbots / Q&A Systems**
- `Generate replies to user questions`.
- Example: Q: *What do you work as?* → A: *I am an engineer.*

---

## 3. Creative Applications
### **Music Generation**
- `Train networks on a style of music and generate new compositions`.

---

## Key Takeaway
Deep learning powers a wide range of modern AI applications:
- **Vision**: detection, translation, super-resolution, segmentation, colorization.  
- **Language**: translation, question answering, chatbots.  
- **Creativity**: generating art, images, and even music.
